MindMatters is dedicated to making cognitive science and psychology accessible to students and teens.

**About the Founder**

Leo Sachs is a junior at Piedmont High School captivated by neuroscience, psychology, and the science of human health. He created MindMatters to make cognitive science accessible to students and teens — breaking down research, therapy, and mental health into concepts anyone can understand. When he's not exploring psychology, you'll find him rock climbing, playing guitar with the Troubadours, or cooking gourmet meals.
